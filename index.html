Tips for creating innovative
collage images

Post by Admin|others|No comments

​Great collagists have a large toolkit of technical skills,
but they’re often driven by concepts.
To avoid a collage that’s too chaotic and disordered,
start with a crystal-clear idea. What is your collage
about? Especially when we’re talking about stock,
it’s essential that your picture conveys a
message right off the bat. “My best-selling
collages are simple, but there is a depth and an idea
behind them,” photographer ​Burcu Avsar​ tells us
. “What makes successful collage is the thought
behind it and how well it is executed.”

Tips on Capturing Instagram-Worthy
Photos in Cafes

Post by Admin|others|No comments

Order photo-ready food and drink combinations.
Nobody wants to like, share, comment, or
use an image of a boring food or drink item
. Keep it interesting. The first step in
capturing a beautiful image in a cafe is photographing
a beautiful subject. An easy go-to in a cafe
is ordering a latte, especially if you know
the cafe is known for unique latte art. Next, look for special
ingredients. Is there a lavender latte on the menu? A Turkish coffee
pour-over? Unique elements are what cause a viewer
to stop, stare, and engage with an image.
